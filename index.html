Ahilya Bai Holkar(31 May 1725-13 August 1795)was the heritary noble queen of the Maratha Empire,in early modern India.She established Maheshwar( in Madhya Pradesh) as the seat of Holkar Dynasty.After the demise of her husband Khande Rao Holkar and father-in-law Malhar Rao Holkar,Ahilya Bai herself undertook the affairs of Holkar Dynasty.She defended the Malwa state against intruders and personally led armies into battle,with Tukoji Rao Holkar as her military commander
